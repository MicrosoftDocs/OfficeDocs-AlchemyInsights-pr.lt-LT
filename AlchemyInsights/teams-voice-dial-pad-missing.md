- Įgalinkite [įmonės balso](https://docs.microsoft.com/skypeforbusiness/skype-for-business-hybrid-solutions/plan-your-phone-system-cloud-pbx-solution/enable-users-for-enterprise-voice-online-and-phone-system-voicemail#to-enable-your-users-for-phone-system-in-office-365-voice-and-voicemail)vartotojus.
