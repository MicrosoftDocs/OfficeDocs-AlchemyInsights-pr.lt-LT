1. <span data-ttu-id="b1e4c-104">[Atkurkite vartotojo slaptažodį](https://support.office.com/article/7a5d073b-7fae-4aa5-8f96-9ecd041aba9c) nedelsiant.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="b1e4c-104">[Reset the user's password](https://support.office.com/article/7a5d073b-7fae-4aa5-8f96-9ecd041aba9c) immediately.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="b1e4c-105">Galutiniam vartotojui naujo slaptažodžio neperduokite el. paštu.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="b1e4c-105">Do not communicate the new password through email to the end user.</span></span>
