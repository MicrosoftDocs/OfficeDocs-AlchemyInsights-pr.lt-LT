Įdiegti, sąranka ir sinchronizuoti "OneDrive" arba "SharePoint" failus "Mac" su instrukcijomis iš [sinchronizuoti failus su "OneDrive" sinchronizavimo klientas Mac OS X](https://support.office.com/article/sync-files-with-the-onedrive-sync-client-on-mac-os-x-d11b9f29-00bb-4172-be39-997da46f913f).
